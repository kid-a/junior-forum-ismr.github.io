The Holistic Forum of Medical Robotics Junior Professors @ ISMR 2022

A workshop to bring together junior professors in the field of medical robotics.

Workshop at a Glance

The workshop has two main themes, namely, surgical and rehabilitation robotics. We aim to provide a venue where Assistant and junior Associate Professors from these two fields of medical robotics can interact, brainstorm, network, and establish new interdisciplinary collaborations. Furthermore, this workshop will bring together senior professors from to provide advice on research and career path in a roundtable discussion.
